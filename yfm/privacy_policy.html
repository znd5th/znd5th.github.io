Privacy Policy – Yatsy for me

Last updated: 3 February 2026

Overview

Yatsy for me is a dice game that runs on the user’s device. The app uses Apple’s cloud services to synchronize high scores and settings between your own devices but does not collect personal data for external use by the developer or third parties.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data with the developer or any third-party companies.

• No analytics services are used

• No third-party SDKs are included

• No user accounts are required

• No advertising or tracking is implemented

Data Storage and Synchronization

The app stores and synchronizes the following information:

• Player name (used for score display)

• Local high score list (Top-10)

• User preferences (e.g., sorting settings)

How it is stored:

1 Local Storage: Data is stored locally on the device using UserDefaults.

2 iCloud Synchronization: To provide a seamless experience, these data points are synchronized between your devices using Apple’s iCloud Key-Value Store.

3 Data Control: You can reset or delete your high scores and player name directly from the app’s user interface. All data is also removed if you delete the app and its associated data from your iCloud account.

Network and Connectivity

The app communicates only with Apple’s iCloud servers for data synchronization. It does not communicate with any external servers or perform background network activity for other purposes.

Encryption

All data synchronization is handled by Apple’s system-level encryption. The app does not implement custom encryption algorithms.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
