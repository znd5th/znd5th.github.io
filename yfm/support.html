Troubleshooting

Try these steps first:

  • Force close the app and reopen it.
  • Restart your iPhone or iPad.
  • Make sure you are on the latest iOS version available for your device.
  • Update the app to the newest version from the App Store.

Common Questions

  • The app is slow or freezes: Please restart the app and your device, then try again.
  • Scores or games seem wrong: Please verify the game rules selected in the app settings.
  • I found a bug: Please email us with a short description and your device model + iOS version.
