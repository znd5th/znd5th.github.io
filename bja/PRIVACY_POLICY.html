Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-04

This Privacy Policy describes how Reijo J. (individual developer) ("Developer") collects, uses, and handles information for the Blackjack app ("App").

Summary

The App does not require an account. It stores game data locally on your device and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Information The App Uses

The App uses the following data stored locally on your device:

1. Player nickname

2. Game results and top scores

3. Basic gameplay state such as chips and rounds

This data is saved to your device using local storage and is not transmitted off the device by the App.

Information The App Does Not Collect

The App does not collect or transmit:

1. Personal identifiers such as name, email, phone number, or address

2. Precise location data

3. Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data

4. Device identifiers for tracking

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require account registration or provide communication features.

Data Sharing

The App does not share data with third parties because it does not transmit data off the device.

Data Retention

Game data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear the App’s data.

Security

Local data is stored using standard platform storage on your device. No data is transmitted by the App.

Changes To This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be made available with a new effective date.
